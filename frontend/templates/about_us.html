About EventLinker

EventLinker is an integrated event-networking platform designed to transform how attendees connect, interact, and engage during events. Built with simplicity, speed, and real-time connectivity at its core, EventLinker helps organizers create seamless event experiences while empowering attendees to build meaningful professional and social connections.

What We Do

  • For Organizers: A centralized dashboard to manage attendees and engagement.
  • Instant QR Onboarding: Simple, fast, and efficient check-ins.
  • For Attendees: A clean, user-friendly way to connect professionally.
  • Smart Digital Identity: QR-powered profiles that enable instant networking.

Why EventLinker?

  • Simple & fast onboarding
  • Smart networking tools
  • Organized event flow
  • Modern, professional UI
